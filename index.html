About Us

Led by Ardiyansyah Yatim, Ph.D, Sustainable Building and Environmental Thermal Systems is a research group that focuses on building-related technologies. Our research addresses the physical processes affecting energy consumption, thermal environments, and air quality in sustainable buildings.
